Book Exchange Club

A transaction-first prototype for exchanging, lending, and delivering books inside a club.

21020058 Trương Tuấn Dũng
Team: ĐT2.N15
Objective and Scope

Turn informal book exchange into a controlled transaction workflow.

The app connects members, but the real value is enforcing the rules around who participates, when points move, and what remains visible afterwards.

20 starting points per member
account baseline
2 exchange modes: permanent or loan
business scope

owner, requester, courier
1 archive record after completion
transparent outcome
The Core Problem

Discovery is easy. Trust is the real workflow.

If it is only a catalog

Important decisions stay outside the system

  • Role agreement happens through informal messages.
  • Delivery status is ambiguous after people meet.
  • Point spending can exceed what a member can afford.
  • No shared record explains why points changed.
Our prototype

The transaction becomes the source of truth

  • Applications and approvals attach roles to the transaction.
  • Physical handoff is confirmed by the correct two people.
  • Budget is reserved before a requester can overcommit.
  • Archive preserves the final roles, status, and point deltas.

Transaction State - Open

Open means negotiable, not uncontrolled.

Role formation

  • Apply as requester or courier.
  • Withdraw a pending application.
  • Accept or replace a participant.
  • Leave or remove accepted requester/courier.

Chatbox workflow

  • Participants discuss timing and delivery method.
  • Owner can choose direct handoff or courier route.
  • Unread counts keep chat and notifications visible.

Business constraints

  • Owner cannot apply to their own transaction.
  • Requester and courier must be different members.
  • Requester must have enough available points.
Transaction State - Locked

Locked means the book is moving in the real world.

01

First handoff confirmed

Owner meets requester directly, or owner meets courier for pickup.

->
02

Transaction locks

Pending join requests are deleted and accepted roles stop changing.

->
03

Delivery continues

For courier route, the system waits for courier-requester delivery confirmation.

->
04

No point movement yet

Points apply only after both required confirmation flags are true.

The lock protects the handoff from late applicants, role swaps, and duplicate completion attempts.

Mechanism 2 - Budget Anticipation

Available points prevent worst-case overspending before it happens.

available = current points - reserved exposure
reserved = accepted requester commitments + pending requester applications
can request = available >= required cost

  • Permanent request needs 10: blocked.
  • Loan request needs 5: allowed.

The design protects the system from the worst case where every pending request is accepted.

Mechanism 3 - Archive

Archive turns completion into a shared receipt.

Owner view
  • Which requester received or borrowed the book.
  • Whether delivery was direct or through a courier.
  • Why the owner earned +10 or +5 points.
Requester view
  • Which book was completed and no longer open.
  • Which handoff path was confirmed.
  • Why the requester spent -10 or -5 points.
Courier view
  • Which delivery they completed.
  • Pickup and delivery confirmations are preserved.
  • Why the courier earned +2 points.

After archive, the chatbox is no longer a negotiation space. It becomes evidence.
